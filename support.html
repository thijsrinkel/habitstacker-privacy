Frequently asked questions

How do I create a stack?

Tap the + button in the top right corner of the home screen. Give your stack a name, pick an anchor habit, choose which days it repeats, and add your habits.

How do reminders work?

When creating or editing a stack, enable the Reminder toggle and set a time. HabitStacker will send a local notification at that time on your scheduled days.

Why does my streak reset?

A streak counts consecutive days on which you fully completed a stack. Missing a scheduled day resets the streak for that stack.

Is my data backed up?

All data is stored locally on your device. We recommend keeping your iPhone backed up via iCloud or iTunes to avoid data loss.
